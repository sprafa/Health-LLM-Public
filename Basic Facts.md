Rafael Lino
Born 1989 
Has Portuguese and Brazilian Dual citizenship 
Has UK EU Settled Citizen visa giving him indefinite leave to remain. 

I have bipolar disorder, diagnosed since 2017-18. 

Initially took Seroquel SR (quetiapine) and Lamictal (lamotrigine). Seroquel dose fluctuated between 300mg and 600mg daily for years between 2018-2022 due to seasonal variation of the bipolar condition. 

Had a bunch of weird symptoms after COVID Vaccine in August 2021. 

Had to be removed from Seroquel / Quetiapine in 2022 due to unknown motor symptoms, at the time described as parkinsonism, which at the time seen as drug-induced (quetiapine related) and gradual loss of memory. This was progressive but really got much worse and critical in late 2022. Quetiapine was reduced to 100mg and Lithium was introduced at 600-800mg. Lamotrigine was removed. By the end of this episode it was hard to walk around and memory was reduced to 5-30min. But right after removal of the quetiapine, I improved fast. Memory and motor function was mostly after 3 days, pretty normal in 1 week.

Some signs of the motor dysfunction remained, though much diminished. 

Due to crisis in the audiovisual sector, I moved back with my mother near the end of 2023, and became her full time carer. She had depression and a seeming loss of cognitive function. 

In late 2024 and progressing to 2025 some fatigue appeared. This worsened towards late 2025, and was left unexplained. By the end of 2025 and start of 2026, due to salt loading improving fatigue a lot, an investigation was started in suspected autonomic dysfunction. 

I spoke to multiple doctors, including cardiologists in the Uk, one of whom said they had seen this before in post vaccine syndrome. However, the reappearance of motor dysfunction and a "cauda equina" like episode of loss of sensitivity in the saddle area and genitalia led to a neurologist assessment of Functional Neurological Syndrome. This is alongside documented polyuria and still suspected autonomic dysfunction. A recent - March 2026 - ECG Stress Test showed loss of function. Yet in 2025 summer I was doing one kettlebell mile a day with between 16kg and 24kg, and I made a feature film which required long working hours.
