## Verbatim Transcription

10 September 2021

Clinic Date: 10 September 2021

Re: Mr Patient [SURNAME REMOVED] - dob [DOB REMOVED]
[ADDRESS REMOVED]

I had the pleasure to talk to Patient again today over FaceTime for a follow up appointment as part of my outpatients' clinic.

Patient is taking Quetiapine XL 300mg, nocte and stopped Lamictal.

Patient tells me that he had very bad side effects from vaccination on 16 August. He talks about neurological side effects such as lack of sensitivity on one side of the body, unable to move an arm and fatigue and brain fog. He completed all the necessary examination including CT scan and di-dimer which were all negative except for di-dimer whish was slightly raised (not clinically significant though).

He also tells me that he has stopped the Lamictal as felt too fatigued and low- he was unable to leave his bed until 2/3 o'clock in the afternoon. This was done with the authorisation of a psychiatrist in Portugal where he is currently residing. Since he stopped the Lamictal he continues to be fatigued but has problems with his sleep. He does not feel is reaching a manic or hypomanic phase.

He now feels a bit better that the side effects from vaccine are abating.

He presented well –casually dressed, pleasant and smiling. No signs of mania or symptoms reported. No risks detected during today's appointment.

My suggestions have been for him to increase Quetiapine XL to 400mg, nocte to resolve the difficulties with his sleep and restart Lamotrigine 25mg, mane for 14 days to be increased to 50mg, mane – this in order to avoid a manic relapse.

He will discuss suggestions given with his psychiatrist in Portugal.

I now discharge Patient from my care wishing him all the best for a prompt recovery.

Yours sincerely

[Psychiatrist A (London)]
Consultant Psychiatrist
