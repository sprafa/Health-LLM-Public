**Past Psychiatric History**

Mr [SURNAME REMOVED] recalls a first manic episode in Feb 2018 following sentimental involvement with a woman. He thought he could see 'love' and thought he needed to start a new religion, he stopped sleeping, thought he was becoming a genius, started becoming hyper-religious going to Buddhist centre every day and then felt a 'detonation' after which he could see 'love', he could sense 'vibrations' coming out of living beings. After a while he began feeling unsafe with pressured speech and racing thoughts and started flooding the woman he was in love with text messages until she eventually recoiled and blocked him. Denies any auditory hallucination, thought broadcasting or insertion at the time.

He then recalls reading the symptoms of psychosis on the internet and recognising some of the features. He went to the GP who gave him sertraline. This triggered a rapid cycling episode.

He was eventually rescued by his father who took him back to Portugal where he was admitted to hospital for over a month and treated with Abilify first and Olanzapine later (dose unclear) together with Sodium Valproate at a relative low dose, 200mg or 400 mg (dose unclear).

He later had to stop Sodium Valproate because made him to drowsy. He also had to stop Abilify after a month because of erectile dysfunction. He reports undergoing a period of depression when he spent a month in bed. He later started to recuperate going to the gym, he found a new job and slowly started feeling better. He continued Sodium Valproate 200mg, in divided doses until July 2018 and eventually stopped in August because he felt low and couldn't concentrate at work.

In January 2019, when he started experiencing the first manic symptoms he went to the GP and was given Sodium Valproate 200mg, in divided dose but had bad digestive problem with diarrhoea alternated to constipation, He asked for a referral to the public sector but was told that he could be seen only in two months. Then he went to A&E and hospitalised for one night and recommended to start Abilify 10 mg, mane and to stop Sodium valproate in view of the digestive collateral effects.
