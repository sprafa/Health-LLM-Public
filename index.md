- **Discharge Notes:** 5 ER discharges (Aug–Nov)
- **Lab Results:** 14 sets — D-dimer monitoring, CBC, cardiac markers, coagulation, COVID tests
- **Imaging:** 7 reports — chest/cerebral CT angio, brain MRI with spectroscopy, cervical MRI, neck CT, chest X-ray
- **Prescriptions:** 4 imaging requests
- **Psychiatry:** 1 post-vaccine GP letter
- **Reference:** 1 personal treatment protocol note

### 2022 — Continued investigations (23 files)
- **Sleep Study:** polygraphy + polysomnography data
- **Discharge Notes:** 5 ER discharges (Apr–Nov)
- **Lab Results:** 8 sets — hospital labs, cardiac markers, COVID tests
- **Imaging:** 3 reports — abdominal ultrasound, brain MRI, brain CT
- **Endoscopy:** upper endoscopy (EGD) + colonoscopy
- **Pathology:** 2 IPATIMUP biopsies (duodenal + gastric)
- **Psychiatry:** 1 final discharge letter with treatment alternatives

### 2023–2024 — Monitoring (6 files)
- Lab results: lithium, OGTT, renal panel, general labs, HbA1c

### 2025 — Escalation (5 files)
- Lab results: general labs, calcium + PTH, calcium + vitamin D

### 2026 — Active investigation (20 files)
- **Lab Results:** 7 sets — endocrine panel, protein/tryptase/ANA, lithium, cortisol/complement, ER labs
- **Cardiology:** 3 letters — dysautonomia impression, Portuguese translation, neurologist referral
- **Cardiology Tests:** exercise stress test ECG (diminished capacity)
- **Neurology:** FND diagnosis letter, normal neuro exam
- **Psychiatry:** clinical summary for ER + email clarification
- **Endocrinology:** comprehensive test request list
- **Health Log:** fatigue and dystonia diary 2024–2026
- **Prescriptions:** psychiatrist test request
